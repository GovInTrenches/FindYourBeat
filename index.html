Oakland Community Police Beats an OpenOakland app

In Oakland, the Neighbohood Crime Prevention Councils operate in each Community Police Beat.

The NCPC system provides a way for residents to gather and discuss local crime problems with a city and OPD representative on a monthly basis.

This tool helps you find what Community Police Beat you are in.
